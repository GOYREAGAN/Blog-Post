Avoid procrastination.

You can do the following to maintain focus;

Set Clear Goals
Focus on one concept or project at a time instead of trying to master everything at once.
Create a Learning Schedule
Allocate specific time slots for coding and stick to them. Consistency is key
Practice Active Learning
Engage with the material—take notes, write comments in your code, and ask questions.
Passive consumption often leads to distraction.
Stay Curious
Keep your motivation high by exploring how coding applies to your interests,
like building web apps or customizing devices. Solve your Problems with Code
Celebrate Progress
Be proud and acknowledge small wins like completing a project
That will keep you motivated to move forward
